### Crafting System Overview
In The Thing That Happened, crafting is a vital aspect of character progression and survival. The crafting system allows players to transform raw materials into useful items, enhancing their abilities and aiding in exploration. Understanding how to effectively use the crafting system can significantly impact your character's journey.

### Crafting Materials
Crafting items in The Thing That Happened requires various materials. These materials can be gathered from enemies, chests, and other sources throughout the game world. Some materials are rare and harder to find, making them valuable for crafting high-quality items.

| Material | Source | Rarity | Usage |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Wood | Trees | Common | Crafting basic items |
| Iron | Mining | Common | Crafting stronger items |
| Gold | Treasure chests | Rare | Crafting high-end items |
| Gemstones | Specific enemies | Very Rare | Crafting unique items |

### Crafting Recipes
To craft items, you'll need to gather the required materials and follow specific recipes. The game provides a variety of recipes for different types of items, ranging from basic gear to rare and unique items.

**Basic Crafting Steps:**
1. **Collect Materials**: Gather the necessary materials from the game world.
2. **Select Recipe**: Open the crafting menu and choose the desired recipe.
3. **Combine Materials**: Place the required materials in the crafting grid.
4. **Craft Item**: Complete the crafting process to create the item.

### Crafting Tips & Tricks
- **Prioritize Rare Materials**: Focus on gathering rare materials to craft high-quality items that offer significant advantages.
- **Manage Inventory**: Keep track of your inventory to ensure you have enough materials on hand for crafting.
- **Upgrade Crafting Table**: As you progress, you can upgrade your crafting table to access more advanced recipes and improve item quality.
- **Craft Useful Gear**: Prioritize crafting gear that is essential for your character's playstyle and the challenges you face in the game.

### Crafting Table Upgrade Guide
To upgrade your crafting table, you'll need to collect specific materials and complete quests related to crafting. Here's a step-by-step guide to upgrading your crafting table:

1. **Crafting Table Blueprint**: Complete the crafting table blueprint quest to unlock the ability to upgrade your crafting table.
2. **Gather Materials**: Collect the required materials, such as wood, iron, and gold.
3. **Complete Quests**: Complete the crafting quests provided by the game to progress in your crafting skill.
4. **Upgrade Crafting Table**: Use the collected materials and completed quests to upgrade your crafting table.
