## Understanding the Enemies

### Types of Enemies

| Enemy Type | Description | Weaknesses |

### Enemy Behavior

Enemies in "The Thing That Happened" have specific behavior patterns that you need to understand to defeat them effectively. Here are some key points:

- **Aggro Range**: Enemies have an aggro range within which they will attack you. Stay outside this range to avoid unwanted confrontations.
- **Pathfinding**: Enemies will navigate through the environment to reach you. Use this to your advantage by setting up traps or ambushing them.
- **Group Behavior**: Enemies often work together. Pay attention to their movements and coordinate your attacks to take them down efficiently.

## Strategies for Defeating Enemies

### Loadout Optimization

Your loadout plays a crucial role in defeating enemies effectively. Here are some recommendations:

- **Weapon Choice**: Choose weapons that are effective against the types of enemies you encounter. For example, use fire weapons against Zombified enemies.
- **Abilities**: Equip abilities that complement your weapon choice. For example, a fire weapon can be paired with a fire-based ability for maximum damage.
- **Resources**: Manage your resources effectively. Prioritize health and mana potions, as well as consumables that enhance your abilities.

### Battle Tactics

Here are some battle tactics to help you defeat enemies:

- **Prioritize Weaknesses**: Focus on enemies that are vulnerable to your weapon or abilities. Take them out first to reduce the number of threats.
- **Use Cover**: Take advantage of cover to avoid enemy attacks and plan your next move.
- **Ambush**: Set up traps or wait for enemies to approach before attacking.
- **Coordinate with Team**: If playing with others, coordinate your attacks and movements to take down enemies more efficiently.
