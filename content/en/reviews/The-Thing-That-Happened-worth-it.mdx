### What is "The Thing That Happened"?

"The Thing That Happened" is a game developed by Mario Murkl and Zoltán Árva, published by Jericho Game Development. The game is set in the ruins of a post-apocalyptic Jericho, where players must navigate through abandoned buildings, scavenge for resources, craft weapons and gear, and survive against zombie hordes and hostile factions.

## The Gameplay

"The Thing That Happened" combines action, adventure, and survival elements in an open-world setting. Players will need to manage resources, craft gear, and strategize to survive in a harsh environment.
