## Gameplay Mechanics
### Exploration
"The Thing That Happened" is all about exploration. The game features an open world with various environments, from abandoned houses to desolate forests. As you navigate through the world, keep an eye out for environmental clues that can help you uncover secrets and progress in the game.

### Stealth
Stealth is crucial in "The Thing That Happened." The game features a variety of enemies, and being caught can lead to a quick demise. Use the shadows and environmental features to your advantage and avoid direct confrontation whenever possible.

### Resource Management
Resource management is another important aspect of the game. You'll need to manage your inventory, ration your supplies, and find new resources to survive. Keep an eye on your health and sanity, as both are vital for survival.
