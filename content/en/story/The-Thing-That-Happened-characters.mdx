## Introduction
In The Thing That Happened, players navigate the post-apocalyptic world of Jericho, encountering a diverse cast of characters that shape the narrative and gameplay. This guide delves into the main characters, their roles, relationships, and story relevance, helping players understand the world and their place within it.

---

### Marcus Draven
**Role**: Protagonist

**Description**: Marcus Draven is the main protagonist of The Thing That Happened. He arrives in Jericho as a scavenger, seeking resources and survival in the ruins. As the story progresses, Marcus becomes a central figure in the struggle for survival, making crucial decisions that affect the fate of Jericho's survivors.

**Relationships**: Marcus interacts with various characters throughout the game, including the Jericho Survivors, Gang Leaders, and Mysterious Stranger. His relationships evolve as he makes choices and faces challenges.

---

### Jericho Survivors
**Role**: Allies

**Description**: The Jericho Survivors are groups of civilians who have banded together to survive in the ruins of Jericho. They represent potential allies and sources of information, but also have their own agendas and struggles.

**Relationships**: Marcus interacts with the Jericho Survivors, forming alliances and facing conflicts. Their relationships with each other and Marcus are complex, influenced by the harsh realities of survival.

---

### Gang Leaders
**Role**: Enemies

**Description**: Gang Leaders control dangerous groups across the wasteland, competing for resources and threatening survivor communities. They are ruthless and aggressive, presenting a significant threat to Marcus and the Jericho Survivors.

**Relationships**: Marcus encounters Gang Leaders as enemies, often engaging in combat. Their motivations and strategies are influenced by the power struggles within their own groups.

---

### Traders
**Role**: Neutral

**Description**: Traders are resource-focused characters who exchange valuable supplies, weapons, and survival equipment with explorers traveling through Jericho. They provide a means for Marcus to trade and improve his equipment.

**Relationships**: Traders are neutral entities, focusing on their business. Marcus can interact with them to gain resources and equipment, but must be cautious of their intentions.

---

### Mysterious Stranger
**Role**: Mysterious Ally

**Description**: The Mysterious Stranger is a hidden figure connected to unknown events in the ruins of Jericho. Their knowledge may reveal deeper secrets about the world and the events that led to the collapse.

**Relationships**: The Mysterious Stranger's relationship with Marcus is enigmatic. They provide valuable information and guidance, but their true intentions and background remain a mystery.

---

### Allied Fighters
**Role**: Allies

**Description**: Allied Fighters are combat-capable survivors who assist against dangerous enemies and help strengthen resistance against hostile forces. They are valuable allies in combat situations.

**Relationships**: Marcus can form alliances with Allied Fighters, working together to overcome challenges and protect Jericho.

---

## Conclusion
Understanding the characters in The Thing That Happened is crucial for players to navigate the world and make informed decisions. The diverse cast of characters adds depth to the narrative and gameplay, providing a rich and immersive experience. Whether facing enemies, forming alliances, or uncovering secrets, players will encounter a variety of characters that shape their journey in Jericho.
